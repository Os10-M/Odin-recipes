Brownie

This recipe is one that I tinkered with while working as a chef, it is my personal favorite. The brownies are moist, dense, and superbly chocolaty.

This recipe will fill an 8x8 pan for an inch and a half thick brownie. Pending on serving size you could have 1 generous brownie or 9 modest brownies :)

Ingredient list:

  • 1/2c All-purpose flour
  • 3/4c Cocoa powder --- Adjust to taste for level of chocolaty goodness
  • 1/4 tsp Kosher salt
  • 10 tbs unsalted butter, or 5 ounces ---Melted
  • 1 1/4c Cane sugar
  • 1 tsp Vanilla
  • 2 lg eggs
  • 2/3 c Walnuts --- optional
  • Peanut butter chips, various candies etc.. can also be added if desired

Step by Step:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Melt the butter: either in the microwave or by double-boiling
  3. Combine: the flour, cocoa, salt in a large bowl
  4. Mix melted butter, sugar, vanilla, and eggs together in another bowl
  5. Add the flour mixture into the butter mixture and mix vigorously
  6. Add in the walnuts, candies, etc.. if applicable
  7. Spray non-stick oil onto 8x8 pan - Or use coconut oil/crisco etc..
  8. Spread some flour and coat the greased pan, then tap out the excess into the trash bin
  9. Finally, transfer the brownie mix to the pan and bake for 20-30 minutes. The fragrance will be overwhelmingly charming.
  10. Use a toothpick to check if they're done by inserting it into the center of the brownies. They're done when the toothpick comes back clean.
  11. Allow to cool, cut into preferred serving sizes, and Enjoy!
