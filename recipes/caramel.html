Simple Caramel Sauce

A very easy, simple, delicious caramel sauce for any occassion.

Ingredients:

  • 1c Granulated Sugar
  • 1/3c Water
  • 3/4c Heavy Cream
  • 2 tbsp Salted Butter
  • 1 tsp Vanilla extract

Step by Step:

  1. Add sugar to a heavy bottomed saucepan, then shake it so it is evenly flat
  2. Add the water and gently stir to completely moisten
  3. Place pot over medium heat and cook until sugar completely dissolves ---Will turn clear
  4. Raise the heat to medium high and cook the simple syrup until it turns deep amber in color ---~10 minutes
  5. Remove pan from heat and immediately add the heavy cream ---Caramel will likely sieze, do not fret
  6. Add Butter and stir until the caramel dissolves again completely ---May need to put back on heat briefly, stirring constantly until smooth
  7. Let the caramel cool and Enjoy!
