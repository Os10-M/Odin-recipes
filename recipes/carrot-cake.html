Carrot Cake with Cream cheese frosting

This carrot cake recipe will deliver at any gathering, follow these steps to take your guests to an unfathomable paradise:

Ingredients:

A:

  • 2c Flour
  • 2 tsp Baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp Salt
  • 2 tsp Cinnamon

B:

  • 3 Lg Eggs
  • 2c Sugar
  • 3/4c Vegetable oil
  • 3/4c Buttermilk
  • 2 tsp Vanilla extract

C:

  • 2c Grated carrots
  • 1.5c Flaked coconut
  • Crushed walnuts ---Optional

Step by Step:

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees
  2. Combine A in a bowl
  3. Mix B together in a bowl
  4. Mix C together in a bowl
  5. Combine A into B and mix until all lumps are gone and batter is smooth
  6. Fold C into batter mixture
  7. Grease 2 - 9x9 pans and coat with flour. Tap out excess into trash bin
  8. Evenly distribute batter into the pans
  9. Bake for 25-30 minutes; rotate pans midway through ---They're done when toothpick test comes out clean
  10. Cool in pans for 15 minutes, then remove from pans and allow to cool completely on wire racks
  11. Once cooled, Cut the crown off each cake layer
  12. Spread a modest layer of frosting onto the top of 1 cake. Stack other the other cake onto frosted cake.
  13. Spread a thin layer of frosting onto entire cake and set in the fridge until frosting has hardened---This is "setting the crumb", which stops any bits of cake from getting into the frosting when spreading the final bit of frosting
  14. Finally, remove cake from fridge and spread the rest of the frosting evenly across the entire cake.
  15. Slice it up & Enjoy!
