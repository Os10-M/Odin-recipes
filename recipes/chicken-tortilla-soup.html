Chicken Tortilla Soup

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/4 qt Chicken Broth
  • 2 tbsp Minced Garlic
  • 1 small onion ---Diced
  • 1c Cilantro ---Chopped
  • 3 Jalapenos, seeded ---Diced or Sliced
  • 1 - 14oz can of Black Beans ---Rinsed
  • 1 - 14oz can of Roasted tomatoes
  • 1 Lime ---Quartered
  • 1 Ripe Avocado ---Sliced
  • 1c Shredded cheese
  • 3 Chicken breasts ---Cut down into chunks

Step by Step:

  1. Saute onions until translucent, then add garlic and jalapenos & cook another minute until the garlic is fragrant
  2. Add Broth, beans, and tomatoes: then bring to a boil
  3. Add Chicken and cook thoroughly ---~20-30 minutes
  4. Once chicken is cooked, reduce to medium heat and bring to a simmer. Add salt and pepper to taste
  5. Top with Cilantro, lime, avocado, and shredded cheese
  6. Enjoy!
